About

Map of Zodiac is an online multiplayer turn based strategy game inspired by classic board games. Your objective in MoZ is to defeat your opponent, a player just like you. To do that you will utilize card-like objects called "Runes". These runes have a unique property to combine with one another, and gain new abilities. You can view detailed or short descriptions of Runes and learn about their combinations with other runes. The game is in early alpha, early builds might still be unstable.
